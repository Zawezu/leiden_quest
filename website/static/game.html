Welcome to LeidenQuest!

In this game you will be able to explore the city of Leiden.

Your character will be a green dot. Click on any white circle to move to that position in the map.

There are 3 main landmarks to explore currently implemented. The red arrow near your character will act as a guide to take you to each of them.

As you move around the city, you will discover points of interest such as restaurants and poems. Click on their markers to view information about them.

The progress bar displays the percentage of the total number of points of interests and landmarks you have discovered
